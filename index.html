UI Research Project

UI 1

Orbfarm

information

  • When opening this webpage for the first time, the instruction of the webpage pops up, forming a small tutorial that reveals the role and intention of the website, and the guidance is very clear.
  • The website is filled with animations that simulate time changes. Whether it’s animation or tool menus, the colors are very soft and will not make the viewer feel uncomfortable. Meanwhile, a high saturation blue is used at the top of the information box, which can maximize the viewer's attention. The middle operation area is made with a circle slightly brighter than the background, which is more obvious when the background is darker, and it is a bit difficult to see when the background is brighter.
  • Proximity is used to make different contents of the same level into buttons of similar sizes, and then put them together, distinguish the contents of buttons with different colors. The fast forward key, reset and info buttons use grey color and smaller size to distinguish them from other contents below, which is clear and tidy.

mapping

Button is used as a medium for users to interact with the website. Button is a good hint that users can interact with the website through clicks. Users use drag and drop to add different elements to this ecosystem, but there is not much guidance after clicking the button, and users may be confused at this time. Perhaps changing the shape of cursor can guide the user more clearly.

characterisation

There are two water fleas under the guide box that pops up when the user opens the web page for the first time, showing the theme and characteristic of the website. The test uses pixel style to unify it with the style of the web page. The pixel style adds game features to the website, allowing the website to make users feel interested and curious. They might be more willing to explore the webpage. The website icon is a pixel aquarium, which also links to the content of the website.

feedback

When the user clicks the button, the button quickly changes color and its appearance changes from raised to concave, then remains, giving the user a hint that this button has been activated. This design clearly informs users that the button is activated. Meanwhile, some buttons, such as the information button, will instantly pop up when clicked, which distinguishes its function from that of other buttons.

UI 2

7 Poems

information

  • The rectangle in the same shape suggests 7 different poems, but the arrangement is scattered. The website's title uses a large font to capture the user's attention. The title of the poem is much smaller than the website title, showing their hierarchy.
  • The website uses three simple colors to classify information, which is concise and clear. After the user clicks on the poem, showing the largest font of the webpage, which can capture the user's attention at once. However, this also affects the coherence of the text, and it is not easy for users to understand the entire poem. It is very interesting and creative to use music to capture users' attention, while giving visual and auditory stimulation to guide users to stay on the content of the website. At the same time, different music is used to distinguish different poetry, but there is proximity between the rhythms of the music, which not only distinguishes different poetry, but also indicates that they belong to the same level of the website. However, users will focus more on the integrated music and ignore text information. Use Whitespace to tell users where information is available, and it can also guide users to click. The website's texture and style are very unified and will not distract users.

mapping

Using only color blocks to represent interactive content may cause confusion for users, who cannot immediately know that it can be clicked. However, after clicking and pause the music and poem, the color block turns into a button-like wireframe, which can clearly inform the user that this is a clickable item. The X-shaped pattern rotating at the lower right corner adopts an interaction method that can be triggered simply by placing cursor on it, reducing the difficulty of interaction. However, it’s hard to notice that this is an interactive item. The design of the Button does not clearly inform users that music will play after they click it.

characterisation

The website adopts flat design, using only color blocks, wireframes and a few shadows to build its style, which is simple and clear. The main components of the page are poetry and text. Therefore, a simple design will not distract users' attention and is pure enough. Bright colors and interactive content add personality and fun to the website. The addition of music enables users to "experience" poetry.

feedback

The fully flat design of the website makes the feedback of interaction not strong enough. Some interactive contents are based on clicking, while others are based on cursor placement with sliding out automatically. This is not uniform and can easily confuse users. At the same time, the feedback is further weakened - users cannot control whether the content on the right side slides out or nto. The music feedback is very creative and enhances the sense of feedback in the interaction.

contrast between two UIs

On the aspect of information, Orbfarm uses pop-up Windows to contain buttons and instructions. whereas 7 poems has no obvious guidance and is more about letting users explore on their own. orbfarm directly embeds animations, while 7poems uses audio to attract users' attention. In terms of Mapping, orbfarm has more distinct buttons with clear guidance, while the buttons in 7poems do not have such obvious styles and are presented in the form of color blocks. By comparing the contents of the two websites, orbfarm is like a game, focusing on the active interaction between users and the website, while 7poems is more about presenting poems, with users mainly passively receiving information. In terms of Characterisation, orbfarm adopts gamified pixel, and its Windows adopt the old computer style; 7poems adopts a minimalist and flat design, greatly simplifying the website's functions and highlighting the poem itself. In terms of Feedback, the visual feedback of orbfarm's buttons is more explicit, and users can feel pleasant when clicking. The visual feedback of 7poems is relatively weak, sometimes a bit annoying, but the musical feedback enhances users’ experience.
